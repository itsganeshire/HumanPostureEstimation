What You Need To Do?

Inorder to use the application you need to follow the following steps. Each step is important so pay attention.

STEP 1

Select the exercise you want to do.

STEP 2

After selecting a exercise it will redirect you to intructions. Go through the instruction of exercises.

STEP 3

Ones you went through the intructions now select how many set you want to do.

STEP 4

After selecting the set click on start it on the camera. Now perform your exercise.

FINISH

It will automatically stop ones you finish your exercise.
